Let's talk product

The aim of this project is to provides people with many opportunities of community service. Community service or social work is a very noble task that has many positive effects on the receivers as well as the doers. There are many people in Pakistan who have the desire to help the society and do something for its welfare but unfortunately there not many platforms that allow them to serve the society on an individual level. The idea behind this project is to create a platform that opens many doors of community service for the people. This platform will not only show the people what they can do for the humanity but also provide them with the path through which they can perform this noble deed. This project is intended to serve and be beneficial for any individual that has an inner urge to serve the humanity.
